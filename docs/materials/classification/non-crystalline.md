# Non-Crystalline Materials

A **non-crystalline**, or **amorphous** solid [^1], is a solid that **lacks the long-range order** that is characteristic of a [crystal](crystalline.md). Examples of non-crystalline materials include glasses, polymers, gels and thin films.
